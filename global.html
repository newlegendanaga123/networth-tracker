Privacy & Data Policy
Your financial data never leaves your device — ever. All portfolio data (stocks, amounts, buy prices, property values, gold, crypto, bank balances) is stored exclusively in your browser's localStorage. Nothing is sent to our servers. No account. No cloud sync. No database. If you clear your browser data, it's gone — because it only ever lived on your device.

What does go to our server: When you refresh prices, ticker symbols (e.g. "BBCA.JK", "AAPL") are sent to our Cloudflare Worker to fetch live prices from Yahoo Finance. That's it. No amounts, no portfolio values, no personal information — just the ticker names.

What global stats collect: Your country (detected from the connection) and a hashed fingerprint of your IP to count unique visitors. Raw IPs are never stored — only a one-way SHA-256 hash that cannot be reversed. No name, no email, no identity.

Abuse prevention: Each IP is counted at most 5 times per day toward global stats. Bots and scripted refreshes don't inflate the numbers.
